CDAT to IDX On-Demand Data Conversion Demo Page

Datasets: We have 23TB of climate data available on our server in Utah, over 900 datasets comprised of 17000 individual NetCDF files.

Workflow: Traditional workflows would have the scientist download individual files for the datasets they wanted to examine. They would have to determine the files that contained their variables of interest over their desired time period, and often be required to download more data than necessary due to grouping of variables and timesteps.

Metadata: Selecting a dataset for the first time will generate the associated IDX volume headers, register these new volumes with the ViSUS server, and return a configuration file specifying the url of these new volumes.

On Demand: The actual data is converted to the hierarchical IDX format only when it is requested by a client.

Performance: Even though some of these datasets are hundreds of gigabytes in size, their respective domains are small enough that conversion of an individual variable at a given timestep rarely requires more than a fraction of a second.
